About

Two screens,
one voice.

I'm a Computer Science & Statistics student at the University of Nigeria, Nsukka, and a builder who moves between code editors and content calendars without missing a beat.

On one side, I build with React, Node.js, and Express — interfaces, dApps, and tools that work the way they're supposed to. On the other, I write and manage social presence for Web3 and crypto projects, turning dense protocol updates into stories people actually finish reading.

I've worked with teams across the Polkadot ecosystem and beyond — Booka, The BlockHive, Eth Enugu, and RevoFi Network — wearing whichever hat the project needed that week.
